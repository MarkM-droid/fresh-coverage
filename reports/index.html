Methodology

1. What We're Tracking

Amazon same-day perishable grocery delivery — available to Prime members across ~144 of the top 200 US MSAs as of April 2, 2026. This is the service that delivers fresh produce, meat, and dairy within 2 hours, fulfilled by Amazon Fresh dark stores, Whole Foods, or SSD facilities.

2. How We Confirmed Coverage

We probed Amazon.com directly for each MSA. For 3 ZIP codes per MSA (near the city center), we searched for bananas and strawberries (fresh produce) and examined the main offer panel plus the "Other sellers" listing for same-day delivery options.

Pass criteria: Bananas and/or strawberries available for same-day delivery. Bananas confirm ambient fresh capability (not refrigerated, but never shipped via standard ground). Strawberries confirm cold-chain capability — they require refrigeration and are the stronger signal for SSD or dark store fulfillment. An MSA is marked confirmed if either product returns a same-day offer.

  • 3 ZIP codes tested per MSA, selected near each city center
  • Both products (bananas + strawberries) checked per ZIP
  • Main offer box and "Other sellers" panel both inspected
  • Pass = at least one ZIP in the MSA confirmed same-day delivery

4. Facility Network

1,549+ Amazon facilities mapped across the US. Key fresh grocery-capable types:

  • SSD Fulfillment Centers (V-prefix): 75 unique facilities — Amazon's primary same-day grocery nodes with temperature-controlled zones
  • Amazon Fresh Dark Stores (U-prefix): 65 facilities — purpose-built perishable fulfillment, no customer-facing retail
  • Whole Foods Stores: 551 stores — now Amazon's primary consumer-facing grocery platform following Fresh store closings

5. Known Limitations

  • ZIP selection: Tested 3 ZIPs per MSA near city center; outer areas of large MSAs may differ. A confirmed MSA means at least one ZIP passed, not universal coverage.
  • Offer type correlation: "Ships from Amazon.com" with same-day delivery could be SSD, fresh distribution, or another system — we are still mapping which facility types serve which regions.
  • Point in time: Probe conducted April 2, 2026. Amazon expands continuously; actual coverage today may be broader.

6. Data Sources

  • Amazon.com direct probe — primary coverage signal; automated browser sessions checking product availability per ZIP
  • Amazon facility network — community-maintained lists (gortofreight.com, r/AmazonFlexDrivers wiki) plus Brave Place Search POI data
  • US MSA boundaries — Census CBSA (Core-Based Statistical Area) polygons, top 200 by population
  • US ZIP code database — 31,000+ ZIPs with coordinates and population (2020 Census)
